What it does

  This looks through the internet for the best deals on the specified item. Not only does it show the best deals including shipping, it also provides direct links to the item if you would like to investigate further.

Why?

  I needed a quick way to search the internet for the best deals on cookware, as doing the search manually took too much time and having a script to do it instead is much more efficient.
